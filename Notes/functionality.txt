1ï¸âƒ£ PROJECT OVERVIEW (WHAT IS DOAP)

DOAP is a web-based Digital Outdoor Advertising Platform that enables businesses and individuals to book digital display screens located across different areas (roads, malls, railway stations, shops, etc.) through a centralized online system.

Instead of manually contacting screen owners, negotiating pricing, or physically visiting locations, DOAP provides a single platform where advertisers can:

View available digital screens

Choose suitable locations

Select time slots

Upload advertisement content

Get AI-based recommendations

Make online payments

Track booking status

The current scope focuses on software management only.
Actual physical screen playback is considered a future enhancement.

DOAP solves these issues by automating the entire advertising lifecycle.
